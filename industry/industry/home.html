About the I4 Challenge

What is it?

The I4 Challenge is a high school level data-driven competition run by students at the University of Waterloo. The name comes from what is known as the fourth industrial revolution, involving the computerization of manufacturing and increase in big data and analytics.

Who can participate?

This competition will allow grade eleven and grade twelve students to learn about management engineering concepts including data analytics, operations research and critical thinking.

When is it?

The first competition will be run in the winter of 2019 via our online competition platform.

What will I learn?

Through our one-day competition, you will be tested and improve on your analytical, time management and optimization skills!

Our Sponsors

Oculys Health Informatics Inc. is pleased to be the inaugural sponsor of the Industry 4.0 student challenge and provide awards to the top three teams. The competition is an excellent learning vehicle for understanding the importance of acquiring raw data, turning it into information and then using the information to inform decision making about flows and processes. The Industry 4.0 concepts are applicable across many business sectors, including healthcare. Oculys is a leading innovator in healthcare informatics and predictive analytics, and has many ties with the University of Waterloo, the Department of Management Sciences specifically, and its undergraduates, graduates, and faculty.
